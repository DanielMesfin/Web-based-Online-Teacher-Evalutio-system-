Our services

This General Teacher Evaluation Form can be used to assess the teaching strategy used to keep the students engaged in learning session .The template contains both ongoing class observation and after class self-evaluation. Use the template to assess if the teacher is promoting a conductive learning environment from the onset of the lesson up to the recap . Next,conduct a short term self-evaluation by using guide questions and ask the teacher about his or her class . Lastly share your best practice by coaching the teacher and providing some recommendation to improve the educator's teaching strategy
